A Rare Opportunity

This exceptional property combines the best of country living with modern convenience. Built in 1979 with innovative passive solar design, this 2,872 square foot 3 bedroom 2 bathroom home sits on 4.6 irrigated acres.

Designed with Colorado’s seasons in mind, this home features thick, insulated walls and passive solar construction for year-round comfort and energy efficiency. The kitchen and dining areas flow seamlessly together, while a separate living room with a wood-burning stove adds warmth and character. Additional spaces—including a den and sunroom—offer flexibility for work, hobbies, or quiet retreat.

Workshop & Storage

The property includes a substantial 30' × 40' insulated shop building—perfect for your business, hobbies, or RV storage. With concrete floors and full electrical, it's ready for any project you have in mind. There is an additional two car pull in garage adjoining the house.

Productive Land

The property comes with established gardens and a functioning hen house. The property is functionally fenced, with multiple separate yards. The irrigation rights ensure your gardens and pasture thrive during the growing season.

True Freedom

With no HOA fees or restrictions, you're free to live life on your terms. Whether you dream of horses grazing in the pasture, expanding the gardens, working from your beautiful quiet home, or simply enjoying the peace and privacy of rural living, this property provides the perfect canvas. Located just minutes from Highway 50, you'll enjoy country living without sacrificing convenience.
